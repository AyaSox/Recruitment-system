   - Check: No console errors in browser

? **Frontend is running**
   - Check: App loads at http://localhost:5173
   - Check: Navigation menu is visible

## Color Coding (in the app)

- **Blue Buttons** = Primary actions (like "Add New User")
- **Red Chips** = Admin role
- **Blue Chips** = Recruiter role  
- **Purple Chips** = Hiring Manager role

## Final Answer

**Q: Where do I add new users?**

**A:** 
1. Look at the **LEFT SIDEBAR**
2. Click **"User Management"** (has ?? icon)
3. Click **blue "Add New User" button**
4. Fill in the form
5. Select role: **Recruiter** or **HiringManager**
6. Click **"Create User"**

? **User Management is NOT in Settings!**
? **It's a separate menu item in the sidebar**
? **Only visible to Admin users**
